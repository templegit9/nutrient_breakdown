## Key Nutritional Notes:

**High-Calorie Meals (700+ calories):**
- Pounded yam dishes typically contain around 400 calories per cup, and when combined with soups, create very high-calorie meals
- Jollof rice containing about 868 calories for basic ingredients

**Protein-Rich Options:**
- Beans-based dishes like akara provide good protein content, with akara containing approximately 13g protein per 100g
- Moi moi contains about 14g protein per cup

**Nutrient-Dense Vegetables:**
- Ugwu leaf contains only 14 calories per cup with over 50% protein content
- Okra soup provides vitamin C, folate, and fiber while being relatively low in calories

**Portion Considerations:**
- Traditional Nigerian portions tend to be larger than standard measurements, with most people eating 2+ cups of staples like pounded yam
- Each ball of akara contains approximately 71 calories
