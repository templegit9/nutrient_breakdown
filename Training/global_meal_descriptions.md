## Key Nutritional Insights:

**High-Calorie Meals (700+ calories):**
- Fast food combinations like burgers with fries can easily add up to a whole day's worth of calories
- Traditional restaurant portions often exceed 1000 calories per meal

**Protein-Rich Options:**
- Chicken Caesar salad provides 45g protein per serving
- Chicken tikka masala with rice contains 47g protein

**Hidden Calorie Sources:**
- Traditional Caesar salad has 470 calories, 40g fat and 1070mg sodium
- Restaurant chicken tikka masala can contain up to 1,430 calories

**Portion Awareness:**
- Two slices of pizza can easily add up to 600 calories and more than a full day's worth of sodium
- Fast food tends to be calorie dense, containing high amounts of fat, added salt and sodium

**Healthier Modifications:**
- Portion-controlled chicken Caesar salad can be reduced to 340 calories with light dressing
- Modified carbonara recipe can reduce calories from 574 to 307 per serving
